⛽ AmEx MRCC — Fuel Milestone Boost

Structure fuel spend as exactly 4 separate transactions of ₹1,500 (₹6,000 total) in one calendar month. This triggers the monthly milestone of 1,000 bonus MR points, worth ₹250 (BRR) or ₹500 (MRR via Marriott) on that ₹6,000 — an effective 4.17% BRR / 8.33% MRR on that specific spend pattern only.

⚠ Must be exactly 4 separate ₹1,500 transactions — fewer or larger swipes don't trigger it. Fuel itself still earns 0 points; this is purely the milestone bonus.
